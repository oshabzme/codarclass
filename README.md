tunde97git add.# 🏥 Hospital Management System (HMS)

A comprehensive and scalable Hospital Management System designed to streamline hospital operations, improve workflow efficiency, and enhance patient care.
This system provides modules for managing patients, staff, appointments, billing, inventory, and more — with a clean, modern architecture that supports both small and large healthcare facilities.

## 🚀 Features
🔹 Patient Management

Register, update, and track patient information

Maintain medical history and visit records

Assign wards, doctors, and treatment plans

🔹 Appointment Scheduling

Doctor-specific schedule management

Patient appointment booking

Automated conflict checking

🔹 Staff & Role Management

Add and manage doctors, nurses, and administrative staff

Role-based access control (RBAC)

Staff attendance and duty roster

🔹 Billing & Payments

Generate invoices for treatments, medication, and lab tests

Track payment status

Export billing reports

🔹 Pharmacy & Inventory

Manage drugs and medical supplies

Track stock levels and expiries

Automated low-stock alerts

🔹 Laboratory Management

Record test requests and results

Attach reports to patient profiles

🔹 Analytics & Reporting

Daily, weekly, and monthly hospital statistics

Financial and operational reporting
